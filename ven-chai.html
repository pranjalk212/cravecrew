About Venkys

Venkys has been serving delicious food for over a decade. Known for its rich flavors and diverse menu, it continues to be a favorite among food lovers.

It started as a small family-owned restaurant and has since grown into a popular food outlet known for its quality and service. We aim to serve tasty and affordable meals to students and staff at Chitkara University.

About Chai Nagri

Chai Nagri is a tea lover's paradise, offering an array of unique chai blends along with snacks to enhance your tea experience. It's the place to be for all chai enthusiasts.

Founded with the vision of providing high-quality chai blends and a cozy environment for students and staff at Chitkara University, Chai Nagri has become the go-to spot for a warm cup of chai and light snacks.
